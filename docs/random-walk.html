2.1.2 Random Walk (RW)

The physical concept behind the Random Walk is the Langevin Equation

The pattern is $ N = 2^n $, where: - N is the # of distinct paths - n is the # of distinct step options (# of objects in each configuration)

\(\frac{n!}{n_{+1}!n_{-1}!}\)

\(\frac{n!}{(\frac{n}{2}+\frac{x}{2})!(\frac{n}{2}-\frac{x}{2})!}\)
